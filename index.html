About the Technology

This classifier uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), a class of deep neural networks most commonly applied to analyzing visual imagery. Our model has been trained on thousands of diverse images of cats and dogs to recognize specific anatomical features and patterns.

How it Works

The AI looks for shapes, edges, and textures (like fur patterns or ear shapes) to distinguish between feline and canine characteristics.

Tips for Accuracy

  • Ensure the pet is clearly visible.
  • Use good lighting for better feature detection.
  • A single subject in the frame works best.
